School Library

The School library of MAAPS is a spacious fully furnished facility with well stocked digital library. It has a vast collection of books , audio-visual material , newspapers, periodicals, journals etc. that are made available to the students as well as teachers. Our library is accessible to all users. It’s a social space for interaction and knowledge exchange and we are proud to offer opportunities for discovery and innovation for our students.

Our school offers various genres of books to give varied exposure to students. Students can consult different books and make academic notes. In this way, they can prepare themselves for examination. Students also use reference books to complete their project works etc.

Apart from academic books, the students have access to a vast collection of fiction, nonfiction books. Our library supplements class work by increasing students’ vocabulary, building on their intelligence and extending their general knowledge. It enables the students to prepare themselves for taking part in the various co-curricular activities of the school. As our library is wifi enabled, children also get to research online to build up on their theory of knowledge.

To encourage use of library and building on reading habits , children are challenged to participate in book clubs , reading competitions, writing book reviews, making book marks, story charts etc.
