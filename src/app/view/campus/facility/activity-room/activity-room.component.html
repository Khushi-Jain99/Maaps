Creative Arena

MAAPS believes in the philosophy of and ‘Hands on learning for its students. We believe that hands-on activities let the students’ minds grow and learn based on the experiences and the environment they are exposed to. Being hands-on is especially important as it allows students to engage in kinaesthetic learning to experiment with trial and error, learn from their mistakes and with the potential gaps between theory and practice.

Kindergarten is a phase of introducing children to fundamental learning through the medium that is engaging, less straining and more recreational in nature. Our activity room is a self-contained room in which students engage in independent and self-directed learning activities.

The interior of this room is designed specially keeping the little ones in mind. Their inquisitive and eager eyes get the feel of things fresh & attractive. It is a colorful and lively room which provides favorable atmosphere where students open up in putting forth their best creative mind.
