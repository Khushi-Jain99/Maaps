SCHOOL CLUB

Maharaja Aggarsain Adarsh Public School lays emphasis on the holistic development of its students. Co-curricular activities form an integral part in the all-round development of a child’s personality. The integration of activities and study is the key element in MAAPS. The concept of clubs is seen as an opportunity for adaptation to life in the groups, forming good life long habits and cultivating a spirit of volunteerism. Club activities supplement academics and give the students practical training which they require in day-to-day life. Clubs provide opportunities to the students to express themselves and display their latent talents. The clubs help students to think out of the box. Various clubs such as Literary club, ‘Cooking without Fire’ club, Eco club, Music club and so on are organised to hone and nurture skills while also instilling confidence to take on life.

2020 saw an unprecedented time when pandemic impacted learning in ways not heard of before. However, to continue the teaching technique of learning by doing, and drive the engagement across students, we took the clubs to the students’ homes. We researched and shared activities which could be done at homes, with limited resources available, and also ensuring that online medium was not a hinderance in driving student participation and learning.
