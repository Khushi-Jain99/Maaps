STUDENT’S COUNCIL

DUTIES OF THE COUNCIL @MAAPS

  • Maintaining discipline in the school.

  • Checking Uniform of Students in the assembly.

  • Checking of cleanliness and hygeine amongst students.

  • Holding regular meetings of the council to overview the students problems.

  • Active participation in the major events of the School.

  • Suggesting the Activity Planner & Awareness Projects of the year for the school.

  • To Abide by the rules and regulations set by the school authorities.

  • Sports Committee Assists the Sports Teacher and Students in arranging various Sports events and Support material in this regard.

School Projects Undertaken By Students Council

RBI VISIT

MAAPSIANS   visited India’s central banking institution, Reserve Bank of India (RBI). This industrial visit was intended to empower the students in Indian finance, understand the history and functionalities of RBI and also to have practical knowledge of the central bank.

MILLION NOTEBOOK CHALLENGE

MILLION NOTEBOOK CHALLENGE In this challenge students were expected to collect paper scrap from different places like school, home, neighbourhood, libraries, etc. besides collecting scrap, students also made people aware to economize the use of paper.

CLEANEST DAY DRIVE

The eco champs of MAAPS selected the site for the cleanest day drive. Students took the permission from the required authorities. The locals and required authorities were apprised of this drive. Students gave feedback performa to fellow citizens to know what they were thinking about this drive. before and after photographs were taken for the assessment of the cleanlinesss of the site area.

PAPER BAG DISTRIBUTION

With an objective to create awareness amongst the public,a drive was undertaken for the distribution of Eco friendly biodegradable paper bags prepared by MAAPS students to the nearby area.

VISIT TO SOS VILLAGE

The visit to SOS Village helps us in developing our emotional and social skills. We saw selfless devotees taking responsibilities of orphaned children and nourishing them by heart and taking care of their living studies etc.

‘SAY NO TO CRACKERS’ RALLY

With the aim of creating awareness amongst the masses about the tremendous air pollution caused due to bursting of crackers a rally was organised by MAAPSIANS . They pledged to celebrate GREEN DIWALI and say No To Crackers and Yes to Pollution Free Environment.

‘EARTH DAY ’ RALLY

To Galvanize Students for conservation movements and to infuse and propel environmental protection norms Earth Day rallies are organised by the custodians of mother Earth.

ENVIRONMENT DAY RALLY

World Environment Day is the biggest annual event for positive environmental action and takes place every 5th June. The students wrote slogans related to the topics of environment viz. water and energy conservation, or saving trees or fuel and took out rallies to create awareness among everyone .

PROJECT BOOND

This Project was undertaken with the sole aim to provide students with Comprehensive water education .We need to monitor water quality; improve water use by developing greater resources for its reuse, and help to raise awareness among communities so that they play an active part in improving their water management and sanitation
