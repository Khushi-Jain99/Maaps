School Houses

One OF THE MAIN STRENGTHS OF THE HOUSE SYSTEM IS GIVING STUDENTS OF ALL AGES THE OPPORTUNITY TO WORK TOGETHER, CREATING A TRULY COHESIVE ENVIRONMENT AND ENSURING THAT AGE IS NOT A BARRIER TO FRIENDSHIP AND COLLABORATION.   IT IS NOT ONLY STUDENTS WHO ARE ALLOCATED A HOUSE, STAFF ARE ALSO INVOLVED. THE SENSE OF COMMUNITY THAT PERVADES THROUGHOUT THE SCHOOL WOULD, ARGUABLY, BE INCOMPLETE IF STUDENTS WERE NOT GIVEN THE OPPORTUNITY TO INTERACT WITH TEACHERS BEYOND THOSE THEY SEE IN THE CLASSROOM, THEREBY ENCOURAGING “STRONGER RELATIONSHIPS BETWEEN ADULTS AND STUDENTS”

School  IS DIVIDED INTO THE FOLLOWING FOUR HOUSES, THE NAMES OF WHICH ARE BASED ON THE FAMOUS LEGENDARY GLOBAL SCIENTIST .

NEWTON HOUSE

EINSTEIN HOUSE

ARYABHATTA HOUSE

RAMAN HOUSE
