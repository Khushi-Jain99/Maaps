Welcome To Maharaja Aggarsain Adarsh Public School

Maharaja Aggarsain Adarsh Public School was established as a recognized school in the year 1988. Our school saw the light of the day because of the hardwork and initiative taken by our dynamic visionaries of the Shri Aggarwal Dharamashala Trust.

Shri Aggarwal Dharamshala Trust(Regd.), Hardhian Singh Road, Karol Bagh was established in the year 1957 . The vision of the trust laid the foundation of our school to impart quality education to the children of the vicinity of Karol Bagh area in Delhi.
