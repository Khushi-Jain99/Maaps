Admission Details

Admission to the school is possible for classes: Pre School to XI

Pre School Admission.

  • Admission to Pre School is done as per the government norms.

  • Notification regarding registration is displayed on the Notice Board at the School Reception and school website as soon as the process starts.

  • Application forms are available on-line/off-line, as per the dates decided by the government.

  • List of documents to be attached with the application form are as follows:

    1. Birth Certificate of the Child

    2. Aadhar Card of the Child and parents

    3. Medical fitness certificate of the child

    4. Passport size of photograph of child and parents

    5. Proof of Residence

  • Registration of your child does not guarantee an admission. It is only a procedure to apply for admission.

  • Attach the self-attested photocopies of the listed documents along with the registration form at the time of online/ offline submission.

  • For allocation of points the school’s decision will be final & binding

Admission to other classes

Admission to other classes is subject to availability of seats.Information regarding the same can be obtained from the School’s Reception in the last week of March.

Admission to class XI

The school offers three streams-Humanities, Science and Commerce to students of class XI.

Allocation of Streams

  • For admission to science stream, the grade points of students in Science and Maths are considered along with CGPA.

  • For admission to Commerce stream, the grade point of students in Maths are considered along with CGPA.
