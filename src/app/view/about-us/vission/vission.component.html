VISION

MAAPS’ vision is to prepare every child to be a global leader who has universal values, global understanding, excellence in all spheres and a zeal for service to humanity through holistic education.

MISSION

MAAPS’ mission is to inspire every child to be physically strong, emotionally stable, mentally alert, ethically upright, spiritually efficient, vocationally self-sufficient, culturally refined, politically awake, religiously liberal, technologically advanced and a team player with the essence of leadership.
